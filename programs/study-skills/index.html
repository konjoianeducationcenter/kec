Study Skills

Studying is an essential part of your school career. There are also many methods and skills required for studying. We offer programs to help you with time management and organizational skills. The regular program has two parts (listed separately below) and there is another one-part program for middle school.
  • Part 1
  • Part 2
  • Middle School

  • The Curriculum: Study Skills Part 1

    The ten-week program, with one (1) hour session per week, costs $1,250.

    Over the course of five weeks, we will follow the plan below, with weekly subject follow up and calendar process:

  • Weeks 1 & 2

    • Intro/Study Skills Checklist
    • Review Checklist/What's the plan?

  • Weeks 3 & 4

    • Time Management
    • Organizational Skills

  • Weeks 5 & 6

    • Note-Taking/Study Guide Prep
    • History

  • Weeks 7 & 8

    • Note-Taking/Study Guide Prep
    • Science

  • Weeks 9 & 10

    • Essay Writing
    • Essay Writing Continued/Skills Wrap Up

  • The Curriculum: Study Skills Part 2

    The five-week program, with one (1) hour session per week, costs $550.

    Over the course of five weeks, we will follow the plan below:

  • Week 1

    • Class Scheduling and Planning (Calendar)

  • Week 2

    • Subject A Strategy

  • Week 3

    • Subject B Strategy

  • Week 4

    • Subject C Strategy

  • Week 5

    • Course Evaluations

  • The Curriculum: Middle School Study Skills

  • Week 1

    • Identifying Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Week 3

    • Modifying the Environment

  • Week 4

  • Week 5

    • Time Management

  • Week 6

    • Making Time Work (Planning)

  • Week 7

    • Building Sustained Attention

  • Week 8

    • Goal Direction/Oriented

  • Week 9

    • Expect the Unexpected

  • Week 10

    • Recap/Review
