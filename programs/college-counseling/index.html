College Counseling

We all want you to make it to college. But the application process is tough. So that is why we offer college counseling sessions to help you with your college application skills.

  • The Curriculum: College Counseling

    The ten-week program, with one (1) hour session per week, costs $1,250.

    Over the course of ten weeks, we will have discussions concerning:


    • College Search and Establishment of a List of Schools to be Considered
    • Information Gathering-Catalogs, Applications, Online Searches
    • Athletic Recruiting
    • Tour and Interview Scheduling
    • Essay Writing
    • Teacher Recommendations
    • Mock Interviews
    • Financial Aid and Scholarships
    • Planning Internships and Community Service
