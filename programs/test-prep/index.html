Test Preparation

Whether it be taking the SSAT, ISEE, or HSPT while applying to private high schools, the SAT, ACT, PSAT, and SAT Subject Tests for college applications, or, even further down the line, the GRE, GMAT, and LSAT for graduate education, we offer a test preparation program to help you get into your dream school.

  • SSAT/ISEE/HSPT

  • The Curriculum: SAT/ACT/SSAT Diagnostic Program

    • The program is a six-hour commitment
    • The first session will be a proctored and timed standardized test simulation (3-4 hours) at our location
    • The second and third session (1 hour each) will include a review of the entire exam with Dave (English and Reading) and Mike (Math and Science)
    • The program will cost $300
    • The goal of the program is to create an accurate “practice” atmosphere to prepare for the actual test as well as provide an evaluation of strategies and time management.

  • The Curriculum: GRE/GMAT/LSAT/MCAT Program

    • This program will have two options to choose from:
      • Option A: (12) sessions with each session lasting one hour for $1,800
      • Option B: (18) sessions with each session lasting one hour for $2,400
    • The sessions will be 1 on 1
